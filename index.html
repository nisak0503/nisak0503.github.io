Learning Plan - 52NLP

Motivation

I want to explore my interest in NLP field, known as natural language processing. I was first introduced to this concept by Chang Liu, a senior who told me that it is the easiest field to start my research career. ╮(╯_╰)╭ Well, it turns out that research isn’t that easy for me to give it a try. During the two years of being a intern or a student assistant at lab, I found myself doing projects again and again without any research process. And pursuing a PhD degree becomes an illusion due to family issues.
After these years of frustration and procrastination, I decided to do something by myself.

God helps those who help themselves.

In this article, I’m going to summarize some of the commonly used git commands that I encountered or learned from Nivra.

1. Keywords: diverged

Scenario:

  1. I new a post but I decide to delete it and not to push it to the master.
  2. I delete the md file in _post but not sure if there’s other modified files.
  3. However, when I do “git pull”, it reminds me that my current branch and ‘origin/master’ have diverged.

Solution:
